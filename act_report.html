Act Report¶

This report speaks on the insights and visualizations from the data-wrangling project done.

From the wrangle report, we learned that the gathering, assessing and cleaning processes was performed on the three datasets gotten from the tweet archive of the twitter user @dog_rates also known as WeRateDogs.

The three datasets were then combined after the various processes (gather, assess, clean) were performed on them to give us a master dataset which was saved into the csv file named 'twitter_archive_master.csv'.

The master dataset was read and loaded into the project using the '.read_csv()' function found in the pandas library. Then the histograms of the columns in dataset which had figures was generated.
